## General Conduct

<Steps>
<Step>
### Respect all players

Treat everyone with kindness and respect. Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
</Step>

<Step>
### No inappropriate content

Keep all content family-friendly. This includes usernames, skins, builds, and chat messages.
</Step>

<Step>
### Follow staff instructions

Listen to server staff and moderators. They are here to help maintain a positive environment.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Chat Rules

<Steps>

<Step>
### No spamming or flooding

Avoid sending repetitive messages or excessive use of capital letters.
</Step>

<Step>
### English only in global chat

Please use English in global chat to ensure everyone can understand and participate.
</Step>

<Step>
### No advertising

Advertising other servers, websites, or services is not allowed.
</Step>

<Step>
### No personal information

Do not share personal information about yourself or others.
</Step>

<Step>
### No offensive language

Avoid using profanity, hate speech, or any language that could be considered offensive.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Gameplay Rules
<Steps>
<Step>
### No cheating or exploiting

Using hacks, mods, or exploiting bugs to gain an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.
</Step>

<Step>
### No griefing or stealing

Respect other players' builds and belongings. Do not destroy or take what isn't yours.
</Step>

<Step>
### Play fairly

Engage in fair play and sportsmanship. Avoid any behavior that disrupts the enjoyment of others.
</Step>

<Step>
### No alternate accounts

Using multiple accounts to evade bans or gain advantages is not allowed.
</Step>
</Steps>

---

By playing on Miners Online, you agree to follow these rules. Rules may be updated at any time,
and players are responsible for staying informed of changes.

Failure to adhere to these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans
depending on the severity of the offence.
